Our Belief

  • We believe in the authority of the Scriptures as the true Word of God, written by men under divine inspiration of God. (2 Timothy 3:16, 2 Peter 1:20-21)
  • We believe there is one true and living God whose name is Jehovah and who exists in three persons, The Father, the Son, and The Holy Spirit. (Matthew 28:19, Luke 3:21-22)
  • We believe that in order to be saved, you must be born again by grace through repentance and faith in the Son of God “Jesus” who by his death made full atonement for our sins, risen from the dead, He is now seated at the right hand of the Father, making intercession for the saints. (Ephesians 2:8-9, Hebrews 9:12-14, Romans 5:11)
  • We believe Christian baptism is the immersion in water of a believer in Christ in the name of the Father, the Son, and The Holy Spirit to show our faith in the crucified, buried, and risen Savior. The water baptism is the obedient response to the command of the Lord. (Matthew 28:19, Acts 2:38, Acts 19:1-6)
  • We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit, the promise of the Father for the empowering of the Christian and its accompanying gifts and fruits. (Acts 2:1-4, Acts 8:14-17, 10:44-46, Galatians 3:14)
  • We believe the Lord’s Supper by the sacred use of bread and wine as symbols of Christ’s broken body and His shed blood, partaken by members of the Church to commemorate together the suffering and death of Christ. (1st Corinthians 11:26, Matthew 26:26-28, 1st Corinthians 10:17, John 6:53-54)
  • We believe the end of the world is approaching and at the last day, the dead will be raised from the grave and Christ will judge all men in righteousness. A separation will take place; the wicked will be consigned to Hell, everlasting punishment, and the righteous to everlasting joy in Heaven. (John 3:16, 5:24-27, Mark 9:43-48, 2 Thessalonians 1:9, Revelation 20:11-15)
